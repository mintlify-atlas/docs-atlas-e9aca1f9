## Legend

Understand the icons used throughout the internship listings:

- 🛂 **Does NOT offer sponsorship** - Company does not sponsor work visas
- 🇺🇸 **Requires U.S. Citizenship** - Position requires U.S. citizenship
- 🔒 **Internship application is closed** - Application window has ended
- 🔥 **FAANG+ company** - Top-tier tech companies (Google, Meta, Apple, Amazon, Microsoft, etc.)
- 🎓 **Advanced degree required** - Requires Master's, PhD, or MBA
