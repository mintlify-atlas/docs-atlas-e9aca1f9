Ready to add an internship to the list? Follow these steps to submit a new internship posting.

## Requirements for Valid Submissions

Before submitting, ensure your internship meets these requirements:

### Category Requirements

The internship must be in one of the following categories:
- **Software Engineering** - Software development, computer engineering, full-stack, frontend, backend, mobile development
- **Product Management** - Product manager, associate product manager (APM), product analyst roles
- **Data Science, AI & Machine Learning** - Data science, machine learning, AI research, data engineering, analytics
- **Quantitative Finance** - Quantitative trading, quantitative research, fintech engineering
- **Hardware Engineering** - Hardware design, embedded systems, firmware, FPGA, circuit design
- **Other** - Any other tech-related internships that don't fit the above categories

### Location Requirements

The internship must be:
- Located in the United States
- Located in Canada
- Remote (work from anywhere)

### Uniqueness

The internship must not already exist in the internship list.

### Application System

The company must use a formal ATS (Applicant Tracking System) such as:
- Workday
- Greenhouse
- Ashby
- Other recognized ATS platforms

<Warning>
  Companies using non-ATS platforms such as forms or surveys must be verified/approved on Simplify.
</Warning>

## Submission Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new issue">
    Go to the [issues page](https://github.com/SimplifyJobs/Summer2026-Internships/issues/new/choose) and click "New issue".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select the New Internship template">
    Choose the **New Internship** issue template from the available options.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in the form">
    Complete all required fields:
    - **Category**: Choose the appropriate category from the dropdown (Software Engineering, Product Management, etc.)
    - **Advanced Degree Requirements**: Check this box if the internship specifically requires or prefers Master's, MBA, or PhD degrees
    - **Company name**: The name of the hiring company
    - **Job title**: The title of the internship position
    - **Location**: Where the internship is located
    - **Application URL**: Direct link to the application page
    - All other required fields
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click the "Submit new issue" button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Please make a new submission for each unique position, **even if they are for the same company**.
</Note>

## What Happens After Submission

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review">
    A member of the team reviews your submission to ensure it meets all requirements.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Approval">
    Once approved, the maintainer adds an `approved` label to your issue.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic processing">
    A GitHub Action automatically:
    - Extracts the data from your issue
    - Adds it to `listings.json`
    - Updates the appropriate README with proper categorization and degree indicators
    - Closes the issue
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Live in README">
    Your internship appears in the README with:
    - Proper category section
    - Special indicators (🔥 for FAANG+, 🎓 for advanced degrees, etc.)
    - Your GitHub username as the contributor attribution
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

If the GitHub Action fails, it will comment on your issue with error details. Common issues include:
- Invalid category selection
- Duplicate internship URL
- Missing required fields
- Formatting errors

If you encounter issues, respond to the comment or create a [miscellaneous issue](https://github.com/SimplifyJobs/Summer2026-Internships/issues/new/choose) for help.
